Why does age matter for AI?
What we look for
💬 Expansion
Does the child add reasons and details?
⭐ Independence
Does the child bring up new ideas on their own?
These two things help our AI guess a child's age better than just counting words.
Why it matters

AI is mostly trained on adult language. We want AI to understand how kids talk too — so it can respond in the right way for a 5-year-old vs. a 10-year-old.

How the AI works
The AI doesn't just count words. It looks at whether the child adds reasons and details (Expansion) and whether they bring up new ideas on their own (Independence). Together, these clues help it guess age better than measuring sentence length alone.
1
