# How Old Did They Sound? — COSI Demo

**Live demo → [cosi-age.github.io](https://cosi-age.github.io)**

![QR Code](https://api.qrserver.com/v1/create-qr-code/?size=150x150&data=https://cosi-age.github.io&bgcolor=FFFFFF&color=0F2340&margin=4)

A public demo for the **COSI Science Festival** presented by the Speech & Language Lab at The Ohio State University.

Visitors read a short conversation between a child and an adult, then guess the child's age. Their guesses are compared to an AI model's prediction and the real age — helping us study how people and machines perceive language development in children.
